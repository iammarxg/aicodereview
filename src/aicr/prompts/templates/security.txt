SECURITY: Flag injection risks (SQL, command, XSS), hardcoded secrets or
credentials, unsafe deserialization, path traversal, missing input validation,
weak cryptography, use of insecure functions (eval, exec, pickle on untrusted
data), improper authentication/authorization checks, and leaking sensitive data
in logs or errors. Use "critical" for exploitable vulnerabilities, "warning" for
weaknesses, "info" for hardening suggestions.
